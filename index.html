Who Am I? My Journey

My name is Bereket Mebrahtu, I was born and raised in a beautiful east African country known as Eritrea. I moved to the UK in August 2004 at the age of 16 and have been living in London ever since. I graduated from Kingston Univerity with a Pharmaceutical science degree. Having comepleted my studies, I decided not to pursue a career in the Pharmaceutical sector as I wasn't sure what I wanted from my life. Therefore, I decided to held off making decision about my career prospect until I was sure. I was forced to take up and a job in the retail sector and have been working at william hill in a managerial capacity.
